Lesson Format & Technical Details

Are lessons only online? +

I primarily offer online lessons, which allows for flexible scheduling and the comfort of learning from home. For individuals or groups of up to two people in Graz, Austria, in-person lessons are possible in the Lend district.

Which platform do you use for online lessons? +

I use kMeet from infomaniak.com for lessons. The platform is user-friendly, GDPR-compliant, and provides a stable connection. Please download and register before your first lesson.

Do you offer individual or group lessons? +

I offer both individual lessons and small group sessions. Group lessons are more cost-effective and also promote communication practice with other learners.

How long is a lesson? +

A standard lesson is 60 minutes. Longer sessions can be arranged upon request.

What do I need for online lessons? +

You just need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with an internet connection, microphone, and camera. The kMeet platform works smoothly in any modern browser ‚Äì no special software required!
