These Terms and Conditions apply to all tutoring services provided by Amy Lang as an independent language trainer.

2. Initial Consultation and Trial Lessons

Free Initial Consultation: Before starting our collaboration, I offer a free initial video consultation (approx. 30 minutes). During this conversation, we get to know each other, discuss your goals, and determine if we're a good fit.

Trial Lessons: Trial lessons are regular teaching sessions and are charged at the full rate of €55. There are no free trial lessons.

3. Contract and Payment Terms

  • Upon payment, the scheduled lesson times are considered confirmed bookings.

4. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations by the Student: If a lesson is cancelled by the student/parents at least 24 hours before the scheduled time, the lesson may be rescheduled subject to availability. For later cancellations or no-shows, the lesson is forfeited without replacement.

Pre-paid Lessons: Pre-paid lessons are non-refundable. The teacher's obligation is to offer and be available for the booked sessions.

5. Termination

  • In case of extraordinary termination by the teacher (e.g., due to repeated misconduct, disrespectful behavior, or failure to comply with agreements), no refund will be issued for already paid amounts. The teacher will provide lessons until the agreed end date.
  • In case of extraordinary termination by the student, no refund will be issued for already paid amounts.

  • In case of technical problems on the student's side, the lesson will not be rescheduled or refunded.

8. Teacher Availability

  • Inquiries are answered within 24 hours on business days.

9. Homework and Participation

Homework and independent preparation are essential components of learning success and are expected from the student.
